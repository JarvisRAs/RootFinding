What is RootFinding?¶

Rootfinding is the process of finding fast and computationally stable methods for the roots of a system of polynomial equations. Methods have been developed to quickly find the roots of a polynomial system in one, two, and three dimensions.

The YRoots package aims to extend the scope of Rootfinding algorithms by applying the methods to systems of equations that are not necessarily polynomials. YRoots also aims to solve higher-dimensional problems. It accomplishes this by approximating the given functions as Chebyshev polynomials, subdividing search regions to narrow in on a root, and applying a spectral algorithm to find the root.
